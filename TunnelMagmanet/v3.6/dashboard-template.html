üü¢ TCP Transport

Best for: High speed downloads, streaming, high bandwidth

  • Fastest download/upload speed
  • UDP over TCP support
  • High connection count (may trigger datacenter limits)

üîµ TCPMUX Transport

Best for: General use, datacenter-friendly

  • Connection reuse (fewer connections)
  • Good speed with proper buffer settings
  • Note: Heavy download by one user may congest stream

üü¢ uTCPMUX Transport

Best for: Multi-user environments, VPN panels

  • Per-user connection (isolated streams)
  • No interference between users
  • Better than TCPMUX for production

üõ°Ô∏è xTCPMUX Transport

Best for: Critical situations, unstable networks

  • Smart recovery for dropped connections
  • No full tunnel reset needed
  • Load balancing between servers

üåê WS (WebSocket) Transport

Best for: CDN bypass, filtered environments

  • Similar to TCP in performance
  • Can pass through CDN (Cloudflare)
  • Edge IP support

üîµ WSMUX, uWSMUX, xWSMUX

Best for: CDN bypass with resource efficiency

  • WebSocket + Multiplexing benefits
  • Lower resource usage than WS
  • uWSMUX: Per-user isolation
  • xWSMUX: Critical situations with CDN

üî¥ UDP Transport

Best for: WireGuard tunneling

  • Lowest security (no encryption)
  • Fast for WireGuard/OpenVPN UDP

üåâ TUN Transports (TCPTUN, FakeTCPTUN, WSTUN, UDPTUN)

Best for: Complex routing, gaming (FakeTCP)

  • Provides local IP for forwarding
  • FakeTCPTUN: Lowest latency, best for gaming
  • TCPTUN: TCP-based, stable

üí° Quick Selection Guide

  • Need speed: TCP, TCPMUX
  • Multi-user: uTCPMUX, uWSMUX
  • Unstable network: xTCPMUX, xWSMUX
  • CDN bypass: WS, WSMUX variants
  • Gaming/Low latency: FakeTCPTUN, UDP
